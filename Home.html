About Us

Saint Luke’s Hospital is a non-profit charitable hospital located in the West Bank, in the city of Nablus. It was founded in 1900 by Reverend Flescher and is considered one of the most important private Palestinian medical institutions in the northern West Bank. Years later, the outpatient clinics were added to the hospital where we provide you with the best doctors and the best medical services in the west bank.

About E-Care

We're here to make your life easier! E-Care is a unique platform that offers a complete solution to both the patients and Saint Luke’s outpatient clinics. It manages the patient’s entire experience at the hospital’s outpatient clinics from the moment they book an appointment to the moment they leave.
